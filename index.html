Hello, I'm Kenji.

I'm a detailed-oriented software engineer passionate about creating applications.

I'm a student of The Odin Projects Full Stack Ruby on Rails and TECHCareers Software developer program. I have experience developing web applications, from simple landing pages, to creating applications that provide quality of life changes by using API's from other services. When I'm not coding, I'm probably out bouldering, snowboarding or just out in the mountain!
